# DM Playbook — Facebook Lead to Discovery Call

Copy-paste scripts for every stage of the Messenger conversation. Adjust [brackets] with their actual info.

---

## Stage 1: The Opener

**When:** Within 24 hours of their comment on your post.

> Hey [Name]! Saw your comment — what kind of business are you running?

That's it. Short. Personal. Gets them talking about THEIR business, not yours.

---

## Stage 2: Quick Discovery

**Goal:** 2-3 natural messages to understand if they're a fit.

> Nice — how big is the team? Just you or you got people?

> What's eating up most of your time right now? Like what keeps you working late?

Listen. Don't pitch yet. You're learning.

---

## Stage 3: Bridge to the Form

**When:** You know they're a real business with real pain.

> Man, sounds like you've got a lot on your plate. I actually think I can help — I built something specifically for businesses like yours. Let me send you a quick form — takes 2 minutes — so I can see exactly where you're at. Then we can hop on a call and I'll show you what I'd set up for you specifically. No pitch, just showing you what's possible.

**Key psychology:** "No pitch, just showing you what's possible" removes pressure. They're not committing to being sold — they're committing to learning.

---

## Stage 4: Send the Form

> Here you go: getoios.com/form — fill that out whenever you get a sec and I'll reach out to set up a time

---

## Stage 5: Post-Form Acknowledgment

**When:** You get the email notification that they submitted the form.

> Got your info — looks like there's a lot I can do for you. When's a good time this week for a 20-30 min call? I want to show you specifically what I'd set up for [business name].

---

## Stage 6: Post-Call Follow-Up

**When:** Within 2 hours of the discovery call.

> Hey [Name], great talking with you. I'm putting together exactly what I'd set up for [business name] based on what you told me. I'll have it over to you by [tomorrow/tonight].

---

## Stage 7: The Proposal

**When:** Within 24 hours of the call. Send via whatever channel you've been using (Messenger, text, or email).

> Hey [Name] — here's what I'd build for you:
>
> **For [Business Name]:**
> - AI answering all your calls 24/7 (no more [their specific pain])
> - Automatic lead follow-up ([specific thing they complained about] is gone)
> - [2-3 more items based on what they said they needed]
> - Morning briefings so you know exactly what happened overnight
> - I personally handle the entire setup
>
> **Investment:** $X/month + $X one-time setup
> **Guarantee:** If it doesn't pay for itself in 60 days, you walk away and owe nothing.
>
> Want me to get you started?

---

## Stage 8: Follow-Up (If Quiet)

**48 hours after proposal:**

> Hey [Name] — just checking in. Any questions about what I put together for you?

**5 days after proposal (final touch):**

> No pressure at all — just wanted you to know the offer stands whenever you're ready. If anything changes or you want to talk through it more, I'm here.

Then let it breathe. No more follow-up unless they re-engage.

---

## Stage 9: Close

**If they say yes:**
1. Send Stripe payment link
2. Once paid, immediately send onboarding kickoff:

> Awesome — you're in! Here's what I need to get you up and running:
> 1. Phone forwarding access (5 min setup — I'll walk you through it)
> 2. Email access (for communications)
> 3. Calendar access (for appointment booking)
> 4. Your services & pricing info (so the AI quotes accurately)
>
> I'll have your AI receptionist answering calls within 24 hours.

---

## Tips

- **Be yourself.** These scripts are starting points, not a straitjacket.
- **Never mention pricing before the discovery call.** Discovery first, solution second, money last.
- **Use their words back to them.** If they said "I'm drowning in callbacks," use "drowning in callbacks" in your proposal.
- **Speed matters.** The faster you DM after their comment, the warmer the lead.
- **One conversation at a time.** Don't blast everyone with the same message. Personal touch wins.
