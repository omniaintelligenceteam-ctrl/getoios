# Discovery Call Script

A structured guide for the first call with a warm lead. They've already filled out the form at getoios.com/form — you know their business, team size, call volume, and pain points.

**Goal:** Understand their business deeply, show them what's possible, end with "let me put together your plan." NO pricing on this call.

---

## Pre-Call Prep

- Read their form answers
- Google their business (website, reviews, social media)
- Note: call volume, who handles calls, what software they use
- Write down 2-3 specific things you noticed about their business (good reviews, busy season coming, recent growth, etc.)

---

## The Call (20-30 min)

### Phase 1 — Rapport (3 min)

> Hey [Name], thanks for filling that out. So tell me about [business name] — how'd you get into [their industry]?

Let them talk. People love telling their story. This builds trust and gives you context you can't get from a form.

### Phase 2 — Deep Discovery (10 min)

Ask these in a natural conversational flow, not rapid-fire:

> Walk me through a typical day. What does your morning look like?

> When a call comes in while you're [doing their work — on a roof, under a sink, driving to a job], what happens?

> What's the one thing that, if you could take it off your plate, would change everything?

> Have you tried anything before to handle this? What happened?

**KEY:** Listen for pain. Write down their exact words — you'll use them back to them in the proposal. Their language is more powerful than yours.

### Phase 3 — Paint the Picture (10 min)

> OK so based on what you told me, here's what I'd set up for you...

Walk through THEIR specific scenario:

> So when a call comes in at 7am while you're driving to a job, instead of it going to voicemail, the AI picks up, books the appointment, sends you a text summary, and follows up with the customer automatically.

Use:
- THEIR pain points
- THEIR language
- THEIR scenarios
- THEIR business name

**Demo if appropriate:**

> Actually, let me show you. Go to getoios.com/demo and talk to Sarah — she's the AI. Ask her anything.

Or:

> Call this number right now: (866) 782-1303 — that's the AI. Try to book a service call.

Let them experience it. Seeing is believing.

### Phase 4 — Next Steps (5 min)

> So that's what I'd build for you. What questions do you have?

Address any concerns. Reference the sales playbook (docs/sales-playbook.md) for objection handling if needed.

**If they're feeling it:**

> Cool — let me put together exactly what this would look like and cost for your business and I'll send it over. Sound good?

**If they need time:**

> No rush at all. I'll send you a recap of what we talked about and you can think on it. I'm here whenever you're ready.

## After the Call

1. Send the "putting together your plan" message within 2 hours (see dm-playbook.md Stage 6)
2. Build their tailored proposal within 24 hours (see dm-playbook.md Stage 7)
3. Follow up if quiet (see dm-playbook.md Stage 8)

---

## Things to Remember

- **This is a discovery call, not a sales pitch.** You're here to learn, not to close.
- **Never quote a price on this call.** "Let me put together what this looks like for you" is the move.
- **Write everything down.** Their exact words become your proposal language.
- **Let them experience the product.** A live demo is worth 1,000 words of explanation.
- **Be genuine.** You built this because you lived this problem. That's your superpower.
